Letter Dash

Instructions:

Click the alphabets before they reach the bottom of the screen. There will be a score board which keeps track of the number of letters you sucessfully click. The speed of the letters will increase as you progress through the game. If you lose click anywhere on the screen to continue playing. Your score will be recorded. Letter Dash was created with the aim of improving the typing speed of people in an interactive and fun manner.

Don't let your letters fall!!!😊😊😊
